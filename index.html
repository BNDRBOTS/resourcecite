Site orientation

What this site is

A free public resource library — verified organizations across legal aid, housing, medical access, victim support, mental health, child safety, and whistleblower protection in one searchable directory.

Who it is for

Anyone who needs to find a real organization that can help — people in crisis, advocates, attorneys, social workers, family members, or anyone directing someone toward services.

How to use it

Go to Resources, choose a filter or scroll by category, and use the contact details to reach the organization directly. No account. No gate on any listing.

What "verified" means here

Each organization is a real, publicly documented entity. Some contacts are still being confirmed (marked Must Verify) — use the website link for those to find current details directly from the source.

About donations

Completely optional. Donations help keep this library updated and the work behind it sustainable. The full directory is free regardless of whether anyone donates.

What this site is not

Not legal or medical advice. Not an intake portal — contact each organization directly. Not affiliated with any government agency. Not a fundraising-first platform. The resources are the point.
